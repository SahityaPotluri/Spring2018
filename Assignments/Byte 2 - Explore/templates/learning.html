Learnings from exploratory analysis

In this project, I performed exploratory analysis on a dataset collected from ESPN website. I evaluated this dataset for the four core properties of Data Quality: Completeness, Coherence, Correctness and Accountability. I am summarizing my findings hereunder.

 Completeness: No missing values found. Aptly answers my questions.
Coherence: Dismissals = Catches + Stumpings. Type of the values match the expectation.
Correctness: No source of bias in the data. In most cases, there is digital evidence available that can corroborate this data.
Accountable: ESPN is a credible organization that provides upto date information about cricket. The dataset can also be verified from news articles or by contacting the cricket organizations of various countries.
